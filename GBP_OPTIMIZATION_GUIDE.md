## REVIEW GENERATION STRATEGY

### Why Reviews Matter
- 46% of consumers trust reviews from the last month most
- Each new review signals freshness to Google
- 4.8+ rating is competitive advantage
- Reviews act as pre-qualification filter

### Review Generation System

**Timing: Request reviews at 3 key moments:**

1. **After Milestone Walkthrough** (framing stage, roof on, etc.)
   - Email template: "We'd love your feedback on progress"
   - Include direct Google review link
   - Offer incentive: "Mention in newsletter" or "Feature on website"

2. **After Project Completion**
   - Email template: "Your dream home is complete—share your experience"
   - Include video walkthrough link
   - Include direct Google review link
   - Timing: Send 1 week after final walkthrough

3. **After "Wow" Moment**
   - Custom moment (surprise feature, exceptional service, etc.)
   - Email template: "Thank you for letting us be part of your journey"
   - Include direct Google review link

### Review Response Protocol
- [ ] Respond to ALL reviews within 24 hours
- [ ] Thank reviewer by name
- [ ] Address specific points they mentioned
- [ ] Invite them to contact you for any concerns
- [ ] Keep responses professional and brief (2-3 sentences)

**Example Response to 5-Star Review:**
"Thank you, Sarah! We're thrilled you love your Brasada Ranch home. Kevin personally ensured every detail reflected your vision. We'd love to feature your project in our portfolio—please reach out!"

**Example Response to Negative Review:**
"We appreciate your feedback and take it seriously. We'd like to make this right. Please contact Kevin directly at 541-390-9848 so we can discuss and resolve your concerns."
