# Local Partnerships & Referral Strategy
## Rea Co Homes - Central Oregon Network Building

### Overview
Local partnerships are one of the highest-ROI marketing channels for custom home builders. Strategic relationships with real estate agents, designers, architects, and neighborhood organizations can generate 30-50% of qualified leads.

---

## PRIORITY PARTNERSHIP TARGETS

### Tier 1: Real Estate Agents (Highest ROI)
**Why:** Agents work directly with buyers looking for custom home builders

**Target List:**
- [ ] Bend Luxury Homes specialists
- [ ] Coldwell Banker agents (Bend market leaders)
- [ ] Windermere agents (Central Oregon specialists)
- [ ] John L. Scott agents (luxury market)
- [ ] Remax agents (high volume)
- [ ] Keller Williams agents (local presence)

**Outreach Strategy:**
1. Create "Preferred Builder" program
2. Offer 5% referral commission or finder's fee
3. Provide marketing materials (brochures, business cards, portfolio links)
4. Host quarterly "Builder Appreciation" lunch
5. Feature agent testimonials on website

**Expected ROI:** 10-20 referrals/month per active agent relationship

---

## COMMON PARTNERSHIP MISTAKES

❌ **DON'T:**
- Ignore partners after initial contact
- Fail to follow up on referrals
- Offer unrealistic commissions
- Neglect to provide marketing support
- Forget to say thank you
- Make partnerships one-sided

✅ **DO:**
- Stay in regular contact
- Close the loop on every referral
- Offer fair, competitive terms
- Provide excellent marketing materials
- Send thank you notes/gifts
- Make partnerships mutually beneficial
