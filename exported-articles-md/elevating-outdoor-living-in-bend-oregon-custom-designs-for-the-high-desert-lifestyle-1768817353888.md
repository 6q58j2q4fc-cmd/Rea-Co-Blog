## The Art of Patio Design Central Oregon

The foundation of any successful outdoor space is the patio. In Central Oregon, where temperatures can swing dramatically, the choice of materials and the strategic positioning of the patio are critical. Our approach to **patio design Central Oregon** focuses on durability, functionality, and aesthetic harmony with the surrounding landscape.

**Key Elements of a Rea Co Custom Patio:**

1.  **Material Selection:** We often utilize local stone, durable pavers, and high-quality composite decking that can withstand intense sun, snow, and freeze-thaw cycles. These materials ensure longevity while maintaining a sophisticated, natural look.
2.  **Seamless Transition:** A hallmark of our custom homes is the seamless flow from the interior great room or kitchen to the outdoor patio. This is achieved through expansive sliding glass doors, pocket doors, or bi-fold doors that effectively double your living space during warmer months.
3.  **Zoning and Functionality:** A well-designed patio incorporates distinct zones—an area for dining, a comfortable lounge area, and often, an outdoor kitchen. This thoughtful planning ensures the space is utilized effectively for entertaining and relaxation.

## Mastering Fire Pit Design High Desert

Few elements capture the essence of the high desert evening better than a custom fire feature. As the sun sets and the air cools, a fire pit becomes the natural gathering point. **Fire pit design high desert** requires careful consideration of wind, safety, and visual impact.

Rea Co Homes designs and builds bespoke fire pits that serve as architectural focal points. Whether you prefer a sleek, modern linear gas fire table integrated into a seating wall at Tetherow, or a rustic, large-scale stone-clad wood-burning pit perfect for stargazing at Brasada Ranch, we ensure the design complements the home's architecture.

**Considerations for Custom Fire Features:**

*   **Fuel Source:** Choosing between natural gas (convenience, instant heat) and wood-burning (ambiance, traditional feel) based on homeowner preference and local regulations.
*   **Seating Integration:** Designing built-in seating walls around the fire pit maximizes comfort and provides a clean, integrated look.
*   **Views:** Positioning the fire feature to maximize views of the Cascade Mountains (like Mount Bachelor or the Three Sisters) enhances the overall experience.

## Beyond the Patio: Expanding Your Outdoor Living Space

While the patio and fire pit are central, true **outdoor living Bend Oregon** encompasses a variety of features that enhance comfort and utility throughout the year.

**Outdoor Kitchens and Dining:** Central Oregon summers demand al fresco dining. Our custom outdoor kitchens are equipped with high-end grills, refrigeration, ample counter space, and often a pizza oven, transforming meal preparation into an outdoor event.

**Covered Spaces and Pergolas:** Given the intense sun and occasional rain, covered areas are crucial. We design custom pergolas, covered decks, and retractable awning systems that provide necessary shade and protection without sacrificing natural light or views. These covered areas often feature integrated heaters and lighting, extending the usability of the space well into the shoulder seasons.

**Water Features:** In the arid high desert climate, the sound of running water is incredibly soothing. Custom water features, from subtle fountains to cascading rock formations, add a layer of tranquility and sophistication to the landscape.
