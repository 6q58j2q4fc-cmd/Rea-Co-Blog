### How Long to Build a Custom Home?

The **building timeline custom home** projects require can vary significantly based on size, complexity, site conditions, and material selections. Generally, from initial design to move-in, a custom home in Central Oregon can take anywhere from 12 to 24 months, sometimes longer for very large or complex projects. The pre-construction and permitting phases alone can take several months. Rea Co Homes provides a detailed and realistic timeline at the outset of your project, regularly updating you on progress and any potential adjustments.
