## The Path to a Solar Ready Custom Home

As energy costs rise and technology improves, integrating renewable energy is a smart investment. Rea Co Homes designs every structure with future sustainability in mind, making the transition to solar power seamless. 

We specialize in building a **solar ready custom home** by incorporating critical infrastructure during the initial construction phase. This includes:

1.  **Optimal Roof Orientation:** Designing rooflines for maximum solar exposure.
2.  **Conduit Installation:** Running necessary electrical conduits from the roof location down to the mechanical room or inverter location.
3.  **Structural Support:** Ensuring the roof structure can handle the weight of solar panels and snow loads.
4.  **Panel-Ready Electrical:** Installing the necessary sub-panels and disconnects required for grid tie-in.

Building solar-ready significantly lowers the cost and complexity of installing a full photovoltaic system later, providing immediate future value for your custom home in Bend or surrounding communities.

## Embracing Passive House Design Principles in Bend

While achieving full Passive House certification can be complex, incorporating the underlying principles of **passive house design Bend** is a core component of our high-performance building strategy. Passive design focuses on utilizing architectural elements and building science to minimize the need for active heating and cooling systems.

Key Passive House elements we integrate include:

*   **Thermal Bridge-Free Construction:** Eliminating pathways for heat loss through the building envelope.
*   **Superior Insulation:** Utilizing advanced insulation techniques (like continuous exterior insulation) to create a highly effective thermal blanket.
*   **High-Performance Windows and Doors:** Installing triple-pane, low-emissivity (Low-E) windows strategically placed to maximize daylighting and solar heat gain in winter while minimizing overheating in summer.
*   **Airtight Construction:** Rigorous air sealing protocols verified by blower door testing to prevent uncontrolled air leakage, which is a major source of energy waste.

These principles ensure exceptional indoor air quality and unparalleled comfort, regardless of the harsh Central Oregon weather outside.

## Achieving Net Zero Home Central Oregon

The ultimate goal in sustainable building is achieving a **net zero home Central Oregon**—a structure that produces as much energy as it consumes over the course of a year. While this requires commitment, it is entirely achievable with the right design and technology.

Rea Co Homes guides clients through the process of reaching net-zero status by combining the passive design strategies mentioned above with highly efficient mechanical systems and renewable energy generation. This includes:

*   **High-Efficiency HVAC:** Utilizing systems like ground-source heat pumps (geothermal) or highly efficient ductless mini-splits.
*   **Heat Recovery Ventilation (HRV/ERV):** These systems exchange stale indoor air with fresh outdoor air while recovering heat energy, crucial for maintaining air quality in airtight homes.
*   **Optimized Appliances:** Specifying Energy Star rated appliances and LED lighting throughout the home.

Building a net-zero home is not just an environmental statement; it's a long-term investment that locks in predictable, near-zero energy costs for decades to come, significantly enhancing the value of your custom property in areas like Brasada Ranch or Pronghorn.
