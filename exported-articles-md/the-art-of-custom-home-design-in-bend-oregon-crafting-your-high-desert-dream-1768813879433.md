## Why Custom Home Design is Essential in Central Oregon

The climate and topography of Central Oregon—especially areas like Bend, Redmond, Sisters, and exclusive communities such as Brasada Ranch, Tetherow, and Pronghorn—present specific design challenges and opportunities. A cookie-cutter approach simply won't suffice. Custom design allows us to address critical factors:

*   **Solar Orientation:** Maximizing natural light and passive solar heating during the cold winter months while minimizing harsh summer sun exposure.
*   **Wind and Weather:** Designing durable exteriors and sheltered outdoor living spaces that withstand the high desert elements.
*   **Viewshed Preservation:** Orienting the home to capture breathtaking views of the Cascade Mountains, river canyons, or high desert terrain.
*   **Material Selection:** Utilizing local, sustainable, and resilient materials that complement the natural palette of the region.
