In the breathtaking landscapes of Central Oregon, from the vibrant city of Bend to the exclusive communities of Brasada Ranch, Tetherow, and Pronghorn, the desire for a unique, high-quality residence is paramount. Understanding the custom home building process is the first step toward realizing that dream, and with an experienced custom home builder Bend like Rea Co Homes, led by Kevin Rea, you're in expert hands.

## The Vision: Initial Consultation and Design Phase

The journey begins with a conversation. At Rea Co Homes, our initial consultation is where your vision takes shape. We discuss your aspirations, lifestyle, budget, and the specific features you envision for your new home. This is a critical stage where we get to know you and understand what truly matters. Whether you're dreaming of a modern mountain retreat in Bend, a sprawling estate in Brasada Ranch, or a golf-course view home in Tetherow, our team listens intently.

Following this, the design phase commences. Working with talented architects and designers, we translate your ideas into detailed blueprints. This involves site analysis, architectural drawings, floor plans, and elevation renderings. Our goal is to create a design that not only meets your aesthetic preferences but also optimizes the unique characteristics of your chosen Central Oregon property, ensuring energy efficiency, functionality, and seamless integration with the natural surroundings. Kevin Rea's 45+ years of experience in the industry provides invaluable insight during this phase, ensuring every detail is meticulously planned.

## Planning and Permitting: Laying the Groundwork

Once the design is finalized and approved, we move into the meticulous planning and permitting stage. This is a complex but crucial part of the home construction process Oregon. It involves detailed structural engineering, mechanical, electrical, and plumbing (MEP) plans, and a comprehensive materials selection process. We guide you through choosing everything from roofing and siding to interior finishes, cabinetry, and fixtures, ensuring every selection aligns with your vision and budget.

Navigating local regulations and obtaining necessary permits from the City of Bend or Deschutes County can be daunting. Rea Co Homes handles all aspects of the permitting process, ensuring compliance with all building codes and environmental regulations. Our deep understanding of Central Oregon's specific requirements helps streamline this phase, minimizing delays and keeping your project on track. This thorough preparation is key to understanding how long to build a custom home and setting realistic expectations.

## Construction Commencement: From Foundation to Framing

With permits secured, the exciting physical transformation begins. The first step on site is preparing the land, including grading, excavation, and utility installation. Then, the foundation is laid – the bedrock of your future home. This critical stage demands precision and expertise, ensuring structural integrity for decades to come.

Next, the framing goes up, rapidly defining the shape and size of your home. This is often the most visually dramatic part of the home construction process, as walls, roofs, and interior spaces begin to materialize. Our skilled craftsmen, under the direct supervision of Kevin Rea, ensure every beam and stud is placed with accuracy and adherence to the highest building standards. This is where the blueprint truly comes to life, whether it's a sprawling ranch in Pronghorn or a multi-story home in Bend.

## Interior and Exterior Finishes: The Heart of Your Home

Once the structure is enclosed, the focus shifts to the interior and exterior finishes. This phase involves installing plumbing, electrical wiring, HVAC systems, and insulation. Simultaneously, exterior elements like roofing, siding, windows, and doors are installed, making your home weather-tight and secure. The attention to detail here is paramount, as these elements contribute significantly to the home's aesthetics, energy efficiency, and longevity.

Inside, the transformation continues with drywall installation, painting, flooring, cabinetry, countertops, and trim work. This is where your personal style truly shines through. Rea Co Homes works with a network of trusted local suppliers and artisans, ensuring access to the highest quality materials and craftsmanship. Our award-winning work is a testament to our commitment to excellence in every finish, creating homes that are not only beautiful but also exceptionally functional and durable.

## Final Touches and Walkthrough: Your New Beginning

As construction nears completion, the final touches are applied. This includes installing light fixtures, appliances, landscaping, and final cleaning. Every detail is meticulously checked to ensure it meets our rigorous quality standards and your exact specifications. This phase is crucial for ensuring the building timeline custom home is met with a flawless result.

Before handing over the keys, we conduct a comprehensive final walkthrough with you. This is an opportunity to inspect every aspect of your new home, familiarize yourself with its systems, and address any last-minute questions or concerns. Our commitment to client satisfaction extends beyond completion, and we ensure you are completely delighted with your new custom home in Central Oregon.
