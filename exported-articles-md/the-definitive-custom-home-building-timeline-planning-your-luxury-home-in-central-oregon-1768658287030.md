# The Definitive Custom Home Building Timeline: Planning Your Luxury Home in Central Oregon

Building a custom luxury home in Central Oregon—whether nestled in the high desert of Bend, overlooking the Cascade Mountains at Tetherow, or enjoying the exclusive amenities of Brasada Ranch or Pronghorn—is an exciting journey. ## When to Start Building a Custom Home: The Pre-Construction Phase

Many homeowners focus solely on the construction phase, but the reality is that the pre-construction phase often takes the longest and is the most critical for setting the pace. Knowing **when to start building custom home** involves looking backward from your desired move-in date.

### 1. Site Acquisition and Feasibility (1–3 Months)

If you don't already own land, securing the right lot in areas like Bend or Redmond, or within communities such as Tetherow or Brasada, is the first step. This phase includes topographical surveys, soil testing, and determining utility access. For luxury properties, site orientation for views and sun exposure is paramount.

### 2. Design and Architectural Drawings (4–8 Months)

This is where your vision takes shape. Working with architects and interior designers to finalize floor plans, elevations, and material selections is an iterative process. For a luxury home, the level of detail required for custom cabinetry, specialized systems, and high-end finishes significantly extends this phase compared to a standard build. This detailed planning minimizes costly changes during construction.

### 3. Permitting and Approvals (2–5 Months)

Permitting in Central Oregon counties (Deschutes, Crook, Jefferson) and specific HOAs (like those in Pronghorn or Brasada Ranch) can be complex. The time required depends heavily on the complexity of the design and the current workload of the local planning department. This waiting period is unavoidable and must be factored into your total **construction schedule Oregon**.

## How Long to Build Luxury Home: The Construction Phase

Once permits are secured, the physical construction begins. For a true custom luxury home—not a semi-custom tract house—the actual build time is substantial, reflecting the quality and complexity of the craftsmanship.

### Phase Breakdown (Typical Duration for 4,000–6,000 sq ft Luxury Home):

| Phase | Duration (Approximate) |
| :--- | :--- |
| Site Prep & Foundation | 4–8 Weeks |
| Framing & Sheathing | 6–10 Weeks |
| Roofing & Exterior Envelope (Weather Tight) | 4–6 Weeks |
| Rough-Ins (Electrical, Plumbing, HVAC) | 8–12 Weeks |
| Insulation & Drywall | 4–6 Weeks |
| Interior Finishes (Flooring, Cabinetry, Trim) | 10–16 Weeks |
| Fixtures, Paint, & Final Systems Installation | 6–8 Weeks |
| Landscaping, Punch List, & Final Inspection | 4–6 Weeks |

**Total Construction Time: 12 to 18 Months**

This range accounts for the meticulous detailing required in luxury builds, including custom millwork, complex smart home integrations, and high-end material installation. Weather in Central Oregon (especially snow and freezing temperatures) can also impact the early stages of the **custom home building timeline**, necessitating flexibility.

## The Total Custom Home Building Timeline

When combining the pre-construction and construction phases, the full journey from initial consultation to move-in typically spans **18 to 31 months**.

| Stage | Duration |
| :--- | :--- |
| Pre-Construction (Design, Permitting) | 7–16 Months |
| Construction (Groundbreaking to Completion) | 12–18 Months |
