The journey begins with a conversation. This initial consultation is where we get to know you, your aspirations, and your specific needs for your new home. ## Phase 2: Site Selection and Feasibility

Choosing the right lot is paramount. Our team assists you in evaluating potential building sites, considering factors such as views, sun exposure, topography, zoning regulations, utility access, and soil conditions. This critical step ensures your chosen property is suitable for your custom home design and helps prevent unforeseen challenges down the line. This expertise is particularly valuable in diverse Central Oregon terrains.

With a clear vision and a chosen site, we move into the exciting design phase. Collaborating with talented architects and designers, we translate your ideas into detailed blueprints. Our goal is to create a home that is not only aesthetically pleasing but also highly functional, energy-efficient, and perfectly suited to the Central Oregon climate. ## Phase 4: Permitting and Pre-Construction

Once the architectural plans are finalized, we manage the often-complex process of obtaining all necessary permits from local authorities. This includes navigating building codes, environmental regulations, and community-specific requirements, especially in planned developments like Pronghorn or Tetherow. While permits are being processed, our team prepares for construction by finalizing budgets, establishing a detailed building timeline custom home schedule, selecting subcontractors, and ordering long lead-time materials. This meticulous pre-construction planning is vital for maintaining momentum and efficiency once groundbreaking begins.

## Phase 5: Construction – Bringing Your Vision to Life

This is where your custom home begins to take shape. The construction phase typically follows a well-defined sequence:

*   **Site Preparation and Foundation:** Clearing the land, excavating, and pouring the foundation.
*   **Framing:** Erecting the structural skeleton of your home.
*   **Rough-Ins:** Installing plumbing, electrical, and HVAC systems within the walls.
*   **Exterior Finishes:** Roofing, siding, windows, and doors are installed, making the home weather-tight.
*   **Interior Finishes:** Insulation, drywall, flooring, cabinetry, countertops, painting, and trim work transform the interior spaces.
*   **Landscaping and Exterior Details:** Final grading, pathways, and outdoor living areas are completed.

Throughout this entire process, Rea Co Homes maintains rigorous quality control, conducting regular inspections and communicating transparently with you.
