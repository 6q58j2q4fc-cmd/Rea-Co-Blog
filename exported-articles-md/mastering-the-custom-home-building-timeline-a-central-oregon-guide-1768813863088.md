# Mastering the Custom Home Building Timeline: A Central Oregon Guide

Building a custom luxury home in Central Oregon—whether nestled in the high desert landscapes of Bend, overlooking the Cascade Mountains in Tetherow, or enjoying the exclusive amenities of Brasada Ranch or Pronghorn—is an exciting journey. However, one of the most common questions we receive at Rea Co Homes is: **How long will it take?**

Understanding the **custom home building timeline** is the first step toward a stress-free experience. Led by Kevin Rea, who brings over 45 years of award-winning experience to every project, Rea Co Homes specializes in creating precise schedules tailored to the unique demands of Central Oregon construction.

## Phase 1: Planning and Design (3–6 Months)

The initial phase is arguably the most critical for setting the stage for success and determining **when to start building custom home**. This is where the vision takes shape, and delays here can ripple throughout the entire **construction schedule Oregon**.

### Site Analysis and Feasibility (1–2 Months)

Before any design work begins, we conduct a thorough analysis of your chosen lot. In Central Oregon, this involves assessing unique factors like topography, soil composition (especially challenging volcanic rock), utility access, and local HOA requirements (common in communities like Tetherow and Brasada Ranch). This early due diligence prevents costly surprises later on.

### Architectural Design and Engineering (2–4 Months)

Working with your architect, we finalize floor plans, elevations, and material selections. For a true luxury home, this process requires meticulous attention to detail. Simultaneously, structural and civil engineering plans are developed. Kevin Rea’s expertise ensures that the design is not only beautiful but also structurally sound and optimized for efficient construction.

## Phase 2: Permitting and Pre-Construction (2–4 Months)

Once the plans are finalized, the permitting process begins. This phase is often underestimated and can significantly impact the overall **custom home building timeline**.

### Navigating Central Oregon Permitting

Permitting timelines vary widely across Central Oregon municipalities (Bend, Redmond, Deschutes County). Due to high growth and complex regulations, obtaining permits can take several months. Rea Co Homes proactively manages this process, submitting complete packages and maintaining communication with local authorities to minimize delays. We also handle approvals required by specific high-end communities like Pronghorn or Brasada Ranch.

### Finalizing Selections and Budget

While permits are processing, we finalize all remaining selections (fixtures, finishes, cabinetry, etc.) and lock in the final construction budget. This preparation ensures that once the permits are in hand, construction can begin immediately without waiting for material decisions.

## Phase 3: Construction (12–18+ Months)

This is the phase where your dream home physically comes to life. The duration of this phase is highly dependent on the size, complexity, and level of customization. For those asking **how long to build luxury home**, 12 to 18 months is a realistic expectation for a high-end custom residence in Central Oregon, though larger, highly complex projects can take longer.

### Foundation and Framing (3–4 Months)

After site preparation, the foundation is poured. Given the harsh winters and often rocky terrain in Central Oregon, proper foundation work is critical. Framing follows quickly, establishing the structure and roofline.

### Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing (MEP) (2–3 Months)

Rough-ins for MEP systems occur next. This stage requires careful coordination among specialized trades, ensuring high-efficiency systems suitable for the Central Oregon climate are installed correctly.

### Exterior and Enclosure (2–3 Months)

Once the home is dried-in (windows, doors, roofing installed), the exterior finishes begin. This includes siding, stone, stucco, and exterior decking. This enclosure protects the interior from the elements, allowing interior work to proceed smoothly regardless of the weather.

### Interior Finishes and Detailing (4–6 Months)

This stage involves the bulk of the craftsmanship that defines a luxury home: drywall, trim work, cabinetry, flooring, painting, and fixture installation. Rea Co Homes is known for its meticulous attention to detail during this final stretch, ensuring the highest standards of finish quality.

## Phase 4: Final Inspection and Handover (1 Month)

The final month involves punch lists, final inspections, and comprehensive testing of all systems. We conduct a detailed final walkthrough with you, ensuring every aspect of the home meets your expectations and our rigorous quality standards. The **construction schedule Oregon** concludes with the official handover of the keys to your new custom home.
