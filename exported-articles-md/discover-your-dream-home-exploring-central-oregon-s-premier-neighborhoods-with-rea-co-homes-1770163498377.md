### Tetherow: Luxury Living Amidst Nature's Masterpiece

Nestled just west of Bend, Tetherow is a prime example of luxury living integrated with nature. This master-planned community boasts a world-class golf course designed by David McLay Kidd, offering stunning views of the Cascade Mountains. **Tetherow homes for sale** are highly coveted, known for their modern architectural styles, sustainable designs, and access to an array of amenities including a fitness center, pool, and multiple dining options. Building a custom home in Tetherow means embracing a lifestyle where outdoor adventure meets sophisticated comfort. Rea Co Homes has a deep understanding of Tetherow's architectural guidelines and environmental considerations, making us the ideal partner to bring your vision to life in this exclusive community.

### Pronghorn: An Oasis of Exclusivity and World-Class Golf

East of Bend, Pronghorn stands as an emblem of luxury and privacy. This gated community is home to two championship golf courses – one designed by Jack Nicklaus and the other by Tom Fazio – offering an unparalleled golfing experience. **Pronghorn custom homes** are synonymous with grandeur, often featuring expansive layouts, high-end finishes, and breathtaking views of the golf courses, juniper forests, and mountains. Residents enjoy access to a magnificent clubhouse, spa, fitness center, and a variety of recreational activities. When considering a custom build in Pronghorn, partnering with a builder who understands the nuances of luxury construction and the community's high standards is crucial. Rea Co Homes brings decades of expertise to create homes that perfectly capture the essence of Pronghorn's exclusive lifestyle.

### Broken Top: Timeless Elegance in the Heart of Bend

Located on the west side of Bend, Broken Top is another prestigious golf course community offering a blend of elegance and family-friendly amenities. The community's namesake golf course provides a picturesque backdrop for many of its residences. **Broken Top luxury homes** often feature classic Pacific Northwest architecture, spacious interiors, and meticulously landscaped yards. Beyond golf, Broken Top offers tennis courts, a swimming pool, and a vibrant social scene. Its proximity to downtown Bend and access to outdoor recreation make it an incredibly desirable location. Rea Co Homes has a proven track record of constructing high-quality, custom residences that complement Broken Top's established charm and sophisticated atmosphere.

### Awbrey Butte: Panoramic Views and Serene Living

Awbrey Butte, perched above the city of Bend, offers some of the most spectacular panoramic views in Central Oregon, encompassing the Cascade Mountains, the Deschutes River, and the city lights. This elevated community is known for its spacious lots and custom-built homes that maximize these incredible vistas. When searching for **Awbrey Butte builders**, homeowners seek expertise in designing homes that seamlessly integrate with the sloped terrain while capturing the stunning scenery. Rea Co Homes excels in this challenge, crafting unique residences that offer both privacy and breathtaking outlooks, ensuring every window frames a masterpiece.

### North Rim: Modern Luxury with River Access

For those who desire modern luxury with direct access to the Deschutes River, North Rim presents an exceptional opportunity. This exclusive community on the west side of Bend offers custom homes designed to embrace contemporary aesthetics and the beauty of the river corridor. As a leading **North Rim custom builder**, Rea Co Homes understands the importance of innovative design, sustainable practices, and maximizing the natural surroundings. ### Brasada Ranch: High Desert Retreats

Beyond Bend's immediate vicinity, Brasada Ranch offers a unique high desert experience. This resort community provides a tranquil escape with a championship golf course, equestrian facilities, and a range of family-friendly amenities. Building a custom home at Brasada Ranch means creating a retreat that harmonizes with the rugged beauty of the high desert, offering stunning views and a peaceful lifestyle. Rea Co Homes extends its expertise to Brasada, crafting homes that embody the spirit of this exceptional locale.
