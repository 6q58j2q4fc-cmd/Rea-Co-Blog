## Why Lot Selection is Crucial for Your Custom Home

Before you even sketch out a floor plan, the lot you choose sets the stage. It influences:

*   **Views and Natural Light:** A well-chosen lot can offer panoramic mountain views or capture optimal sunlight throughout the day.
*   **Privacy and Space:** Some lots provide secluded retreats, while others offer community engagement.
*   **Accessibility and Amenities:** Proximity to schools, downtown Bend, golf courses, or outdoor recreation is often a key factor.
*   **Building Costs and Design:** Topography, soil conditions, and utility access can significantly impact construction complexity and cost. A sloped lot might offer incredible views but require more extensive foundation work.
*   **Resale Value:** A desirable location with sought-after features will always hold its value.

## Custom Home Lot Selection Tips from the Experts at Rea Co Homes

Navigating the Central Oregon real estate market for land can be complex. Here are essential **custom home lot selection tips** to guide your search:

### 1. Define Your Lifestyle and Priorities

Start by envisioning your ideal life. Do you crave the vibrant energy of Bend, the exclusive golf community of Tetherow, the sprawling ranch feel of Brasada, or the high-desert luxury of Pronghorn? Consider:

*   **Activities:** Are you an avid golfer, hiker, skier, or fly-fisher? Proximity to your passions is key.
*   **Community:** Do you prefer a bustling neighborhood, a quiet cul-de-sac, or a more isolated setting?
*   **Views:** Are mountain views (like the iconic Three Sisters), river views, or high desert landscapes your preference? Many seek **view lots Bend Oregon** offers, which can vary greatly in price and availability.

### 2. Location, Location, Location: Exploring Central Oregon's Gems

Central Oregon boasts several premier communities, each with its unique appeal:

*   **Tetherow:** Known for its world-class golf course, stunning views, and proximity to Bend, **the best lots in Tetherow** often feature incredible vistas of the Cascades and privacy. It's a prime location for luxury custom homes.
*   **Bend:** From established neighborhoods to new developments, Bend offers diverse options. Many seek **view lots Bend Oregon** provides, especially those overlooking the Deschutes River, Pilot Butte, or the surrounding mountains.
*   **Brasada Ranch:** Offering a more expansive, resort-style living experience with a focus on equestrian activities and golf, lots here tend to be larger and more private.
*   **Pronghorn:** A high-end community featuring two championship golf courses, Pronghorn offers luxury living amidst a stunning natural setting.
*   **Sisters & Redmond:** For those seeking a slightly quieter pace or more land, areas around Sisters and Redmond offer beautiful rural and semi-rural options.

### 3. Understand the Land Itself

Don't just look at the view; look at the ground beneath your feet. Kevin Rea advises:

*   **Topography:** Is the lot flat, gently sloped, or steeply graded? Slopes can offer better views but increase construction costs. Rea Co Homes has extensive experience building on challenging terrains.
*   **Soil Conditions:** Poor soil can lead to expensive foundation work. A geotechnical report is essential.
*   **Utilities:** Verify access to water, sewer/septic, electricity, gas, and internet. Some rural lots may require well and septic systems.
*   **Easements and Setbacks:** Understand any restrictions on where you can build on the property.
*   **Sun Exposure:** How does the sun track across the property throughout the day and year? This impacts energy efficiency and outdoor living spaces.

### 4. Consider Future Development and Zoning

Investigate what's planned for surrounding parcels. Will a new development obstruct your view? What are the zoning regulations for your chosen area? Rea Co Homes can help you understand these critical details, ensuring your investment is protected.
