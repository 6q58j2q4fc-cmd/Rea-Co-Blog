## Embracing Modern Rustic Homes

One of the most sought-after styles in Central Oregon is the modern rustic home. This aesthetic beautifully marries the warmth and authenticity of rustic elements with the clean lines and sophisticated simplicity of modern design. Think exposed timber beams paired with polished concrete floors, natural stone fireplaces alongside sleek, minimalist cabinetry, and reclaimed wood accents integrated into a contemporary color palette.

Modern rustic homes are characterized by their ability to feel both grand and inviting. They often feature large, comfortable furnishings, rich textures like wool and leather, and a thoughtful balance of natural light and strategic lighting fixtures. Rea Co Homes has perfected the art of creating these spaces, ensuring each element contributes to an overall feeling of understated luxury and connection to nature.

## The Allure of High Desert Aesthetic Design

The high desert aesthetic design is another signature look that resonates deeply with our clients in Bend and surrounding areas. This style celebrates the unique beauty of the high desert landscape, incorporating earthy tones, organic textures, and a sense of spaciousness. Colors often reflect the natural palette of the desert—sandy beiges, warm grays, deep greens, and occasional pops of terracotta or sky blue.

Key features of high desert aesthetic design include natural wood finishes, often lighter in tone, stone accents, and an emphasis on indoor-outdoor living. Large sliding glass doors, covered patios, and thoughtfully designed outdoor living spaces are integral. Furniture tends to be comfortable and durable, often with a handcrafted feel, complementing the rugged elegance of the environment. Rea Co Homes excels at creating interiors that feel both grounded and expansive, perfectly capturing the spirit of the high desert.

## Defining Mountain Modern Interior

For homes nestled closer to the mountains or those seeking a more alpine-inspired feel, mountain modern interior design offers a sophisticated blend of traditional mountain lodge charm and contemporary elegance. This style often incorporates robust natural materials like heavy timbers, stone, and steel, but with a refined and minimalist execution.

Mountain modern interiors prioritize comfort and warmth, using rich textures and a palette that evokes the forest and mountains. Large, dramatic fireplaces are often central features, alongside vaulted ceilings and expansive windows that frame breathtaking mountain vistas. While rustic elements are present, they are balanced with sleek finishes, state-of-the-art appliances, and smart home technology. Kevin Rea and his team have a proven track record of delivering stunning mountain modern homes that are both luxurious and deeply connected to their majestic surroundings.
