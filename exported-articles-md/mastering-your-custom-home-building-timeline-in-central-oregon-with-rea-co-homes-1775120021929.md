## The Foundation of Your Dream: Pre-Construction Planning (3-6 Months)

The initial phase, often underestimated, is crucial. This is where your vision truly takes shape. It typically spans 3 to 6 months, though complex designs or unique site challenges can extend it.

*   **Discovery & Design (1-3 Months):** This begins with in-depth consultations with Kevin Rea and our design team. We delve into your lifestyle, aesthetic preferences, budget, and specific needs. For properties in Bend, Brasada Ranch, Tetherow, or Pronghorn, understanding community architectural guidelines is critical. This phase involves site analysis, architectural conceptualization, and initial floor plan development. This is where we answer the fundamental question: **when to start building custom home** by aligning your readiness with market conditions and design complexity.
*   **Architectural & Engineering Drawings (1-2 Months):** Once the conceptual design is approved, detailed architectural blueprints, structural engineering plans, and mechanical, electrical, and plumbing (MEP) schematics are developed. This is a highly collaborative process, ensuring every detail is accounted for.
*   **Permitting & Approvals (1-2 Months):** Navigating local zoning laws, environmental regulations, and building codes in Central Oregon can be complex. Rea Co Homes expertly handles the submission of all necessary permits to local authorities (e.g., Deschutes County, City of Bend). This timeframe can vary significantly based on the project's complexity and the jurisdiction's current workload. Patience here is key, as these approvals are non-negotiable for a smooth **construction schedule Oregon**.
