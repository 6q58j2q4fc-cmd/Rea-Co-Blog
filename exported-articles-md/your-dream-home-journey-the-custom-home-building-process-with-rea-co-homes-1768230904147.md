## Phase 1: Vision & Design – Laying the Foundation of Your Dream

Every great home begins with a great idea. This initial phase is all about translating your vision into tangible plans. It typically involves:

*   **Initial Consultation:** We start with an in-depth discussion about your desires, lifestyle, budget, and preferred locations (e.g., a sprawling estate in Brasada Ranch or a modern retreat in Tetherow). This helps us understand your unique needs.
*   **Site Selection & Analysis:** If you haven't already, we assist in finding the perfect lot that complements your vision and offers optimal views and accessibility. We then conduct thorough site analysis, considering topography, sun exposure, utilities, and local zoning regulations specific to Central Oregon.
*   **Architectural Design & Planning:** Working with renowned architects, we develop preliminary designs, floor plans, and elevations. This iterative process ensures every detail, from room layout to window placement, aligns with your aesthetic and functional requirements. Kevin Rea's input here is invaluable, often suggesting innovative solutions or efficiencies.
*   **Budgeting & Estimating:** Based on the design, we provide detailed cost estimates, ensuring transparency and alignment with your financial goals. We discuss material selections and finishes to refine the budget.
*   **Permitting & Approvals:** Navigating local regulations can be complex. We manage the entire permitting process with local authorities in Bend and surrounding communities, ensuring all plans comply with building codes and environmental standards.

This phase is critical for setting the stage for a smooth **construction process** and typically takes several weeks to a few months, depending on the complexity of the design and permitting requirements.

## Phase 2: Pre-Construction – Preparing for Groundbreaking

With designs approved and permits in hand, the pre-construction phase focuses on meticulous preparation before physical work begins.

*   **Finalizing Selections:** This is where your home truly starts to take shape on paper. You'll make final decisions on everything from cabinetry and countertops to flooring, lighting, and exterior finishes. Our team provides guidance and access to showrooms to simplify this process.
*   **Contract & Financing:** We finalize the construction contract, outlining all specifications, timelines, and payment schedules. We also work with your lender to ensure financing is in place.
*   **Scheduling & Logistics:** Kevin Rea's expertise shines here as we develop a comprehensive project schedule, coordinating all subcontractors, material deliveries, and inspections. This proactive planning minimizes delays and keeps the **home building timeline** on track.
*   **Site Preparation:** The physical work begins with clearing the lot, grading, and preparing the foundation site according to the approved plans.

## Phase 3: Construction – Bringing Your Vision to Life

This is the most visible and exciting part of the **custom home building process**, where your blueprints transform into a tangible structure.

*   **Foundation & Framing:** The foundation is poured, followed by the framing of walls, floors, and roof. This establishes the basic structure and layout of your home.
*   **Rough-Ins:** Once framed, mechanical (HVAC), electrical, and plumbing systems are installed within the walls, ceilings, and floors. These are critically inspected before moving to the next stage.
*   **Exterior Finishes:** Roofing, windows, doors, and exterior siding are installed, weatherproofing your home and defining its curb appeal. This is where the unique architectural elements of your Bend or Pronghorn home truly emerge.
*   **Interior Finishes:** Insulation, drywall, interior doors, trim, cabinetry, and flooring are installed. This phase also includes painting and the installation of fixtures, appliances, and custom features.
*   **Landscaping & Exterior Details:** The final touches outside, including landscaping, driveways, and patios, complete the exterior aesthetic.

Throughout this phase, Kevin Rea and our project managers maintain rigorous oversight, conducting regular site visits, quality checks, and communicating progress with you. Our commitment to excellence ensures every detail meets our high standards.
