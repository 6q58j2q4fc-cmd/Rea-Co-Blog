## The Foundation of Exceptional Patio Design Central Oregon

The cornerstone of any great outdoor area is the patio. In Central Oregon, where temperatures fluctuate dramatically and the sun is intense, material selection is paramount. We focus on durable, locally sourced, and climate-appropriate materials that stand the test of time while complementing the natural surroundings.

**Patio design Central Oregon** isn't just about pouring concrete. It involves strategic placement to maximize views (like the iconic vistas at Tetherow or Brasada Ranch), ensuring proper drainage, and integrating seamless transitions from the interior of your custom home. We often incorporate covered areas, utilizing timber framing or sleek modern pergolas, to provide essential shade during the summer months, making the space usable throughout the day.

Key considerations in our custom patio designs include:

*   **Materiality:** Using natural stone, durable pavers, or stamped concrete that mimics the high desert palette.
*   **Flow:** Designing multiple access points from the great room, kitchen, and primary suite.
*   **Zoning:** Creating distinct areas for dining, lounging, and entertainment.

## Fire Pit Design High Desert: Embracing the Central Oregon Evenings

The Central Oregon high desert is known for its spectacular, star-filled nights and cool temperatures, even in mid-summer. A custom fire feature is essential for extending the use of your outdoor space well past sunset.

**Fire pit design high desert** style focuses on creating a gathering point that is both safe and striking. We move beyond simple metal bowls, designing custom masonry or concrete fire pits that become architectural focal points. These designs often incorporate built-in seating walls, utilizing materials that echo the surrounding landscape.

From linear gas fire tables that offer modern elegance to large, circular wood-burning pits perfect for gathering friends and family, Kevin Rea’s team ensures the design is functional and compliant with Central Oregon's fire safety regulations. These features provide warmth, ambiance, and a natural center for storytelling and relaxation under the vast desert sky.
