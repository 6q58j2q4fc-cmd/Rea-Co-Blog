## The Art of Patio Design Central Oregon

A well-designed patio is the foundation of any successful outdoor living space. In Central Oregon, where outdoor dining and entertaining are essential, the patio must function as an all-season room. Our approach to **patio design Central Oregon** focuses on several key elements:

### 1. Seamless Integration

We prioritize connecting the interior of your home—especially the kitchen and great room—to the exterior. This often involves expansive sliding glass doors, covered loggias, and thoughtful transitions using matching or complementary materials. For luxury communities like Tetherow, where views of the Cascade Mountains are paramount, the patio is strategically positioned to maximize sightlines while offering necessary protection from prevailing winds.

### 2. Material Selection for Durability

The high desert environment requires materials that can handle freeze-thaw cycles and intense UV exposure. We often utilize locally sourced stone, durable composite decking, and high-quality concrete finishes. For homes in Brasada Ranch, where rustic elegance meets desert resilience, we select materials that reflect the natural landscape while ensuring longevity and minimal maintenance.

### 3. Covered Spaces and Shade Solutions

While sunshine is abundant, shade is crucial for comfortable daytime use. Rea Co Homes designs custom pergolas, retractable awnings, and covered outdoor kitchens that provide relief during the peak summer months. These structures are designed to complement the architectural style of your custom home, adding both functionality and aesthetic value.

## Creating Warmth: Fire Pit Design High Desert Style

As the sun sets over the Cascades, the temperature in the high desert drops quickly, making a central heat source vital for evening enjoyment. A custom fire feature is often the centerpiece of our **outdoor living Bend Oregon** projects.

Our expertise in **fire pit design high desert** environments ensures that your feature is both safe and stunning. We move beyond simple prefabricated units, focusing on bespoke designs that anchor the gathering space. Considerations include:

*   **Location and Zoning:** Ensuring the fire pit is positioned safely away from structures and vegetation, adhering to local regulations, especially in wildfire-prone areas.
*   **Fuel Source:** Choosing between natural gas, propane, or traditional wood-burning options based on convenience and aesthetic preference.
*   **Materials:** Utilizing durable, heat-resistant materials like basalt, river rock, or custom-cut steel that reflect the rugged beauty of the Central Oregon landscape.

Imagine gathering around a custom-built, gas-fired basalt pit at your Pronghorn home, enjoying the clear night sky—this is the level of detail and luxury Rea Co Homes delivers.
