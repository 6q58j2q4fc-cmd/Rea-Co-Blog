### Tetherow: A Golfer's Paradise with Mountain Views

Nestled just west of Bend, **Tetherow** is an award-winning master-planned community celebrated for its dramatic high desert landscape and the highly acclaimed David McLay Kidd-designed golf course. For those searching for **Tetherow homes for sale**, the appeal extends beyond the greens. Residents enjoy access to a full suite of amenities, including a luxurious resort lodge, fitness center, pools, and an extensive network of trails. Building a custom home in Tetherow means embracing a lifestyle of outdoor recreation and refined living, all while being minutes from downtown Bend's vibrant scene. Rea Co Homes has a proven track record of constructing exquisite residences that capture Tetherow's unique aesthetic, combining modern design with natural elements.

### Pronghorn: Unrivaled Luxury and Desert Serenity

East of Bend lies **Pronghorn**, an exclusive private club community that epitomizes luxury and tranquility. Home to two championship golf courses – one designed by Jack Nicklaus and the other by Tom Fazio – Pronghorn offers an unparalleled golf experience. But it's not just for golfers; the community boasts a magnificent clubhouse, spa, fine dining, and a range of outdoor activities set against a backdrop of ancient juniper trees and panoramic mountain views. If you're envisioning **Pronghorn custom homes**, you're seeking a sanctuary where privacy and world-class amenities converge. Rea Co Homes specializes in designing and building residences that complement Pronghorn's sophisticated desert modern architecture, creating spaces that are both grand and intimately connected to nature.

### Broken Top: Timeless Elegance in Bend

Closer to the heart of Bend, **Broken Top** stands as a testament to classic Central Oregon luxury. This established, gated community is centered around an 18-hole championship golf course designed by Tom Weiskopf and Jay Morrish, offering stunning views of the Cascades. **Broken Top luxury homes** are known for their enduring appeal, often featuring elegant craftsmanship, spacious layouts, and meticulously landscaped properties. The community provides a private club experience with tennis, swimming, and fine dining, all within easy reach of Bend's urban conveniences. Rea Co Homes has a deep understanding of Broken Top's architectural guidelines and community spirit, ensuring your custom build upholds the neighborhood's esteemed reputation.

### Awbrey Butte: Elevated Living with Panoramic Vistas

Perched above the city of Bend, **Awbrey Butte** offers some of the most spectacular views in Central Oregon, encompassing the Cascade Mountains, the Deschutes River, and the city lights below. This prestigious area is characterized by its custom estates and a sense of peaceful seclusion, while still being incredibly close to Bend's amenities. For those seeking **Awbrey Butte builders** who can maximize these incredible vistas, Rea Co Homes is the ideal partner. Our expertise in site planning and architectural design ensures that your home is perfectly positioned to capture every sunset and mountain peak, creating a living experience that is truly elevated.

### North Rim: Exclusivity and Natural Beauty

For discerning homeowners, **North Rim** offers another exceptional option within Bend. Known for its large, private lots and custom estates, North Rim provides a serene, natural setting with stunning views and direct access to the Deschutes River. If you're looking for a **North Rim custom builder** who can craft a home that harmonizes with this pristine environment, Rea Co Homes possesses the experience and vision to deliver.
