## Understanding the Basics of Custom Home Financing

Unlike purchasing an existing home, **financing a custom home build** involves a different set of considerations. A traditional mortgage won't suffice for a property that doesn't yet exist. This is where construction loans come into play. These specialized loans are designed to cover the costs incurred during the building process, from land acquisition (if not already owned) to materials, labor, and permits.
