# Mastering Your Custom Home Building Timeline in Central Oregon: A Rea Co Homes Guide

Building a custom luxury home in Central Oregon—whether nestled among the pines of Tetherow, overlooking the high desert at Brasada Ranch, or centrally located in Bend—is a dream realized through meticulous planning. One of the most common questions we receive at Rea Co Homes is: **What is the custom home building timeline?**

The answer is complex, but transparency is key. Led by Kevin Rea, who brings over 45 years of award-winning experience to every project, Rea Co Homes specializes in demystifying the process. We don't just build houses; we craft legacies. Understanding the phases involved is essential for setting realistic expectations and ensuring a smooth journey from concept to completion.

## Phase 1: Planning and Design (3–6 Months)

**When to start building custom home?** The answer is long before the first shovel hits the dirt. The initial planning and design phase is arguably the most critical component of the entire **construction schedule Oregon** dictates.

This phase involves:

1.  **Initial Consultation & Site Assessment:** Meeting with Kevin Rea and the design team to discuss your vision, budget, and lifestyle needs. We assess your specific lot in areas like Pronghorn or Sisters, considering topography, views, and utility access.
2.  **Architectural Design:** Working with your architect to finalize floor plans, elevations, and 3D renderings. This is an iterative process requiring careful review.
3.  **Engineering & Permitting Documentation:** Structural, mechanical, and civil engineering drawings are finalized. This paperwork is essential for navigating Central Oregon's specific building codes.

***Key Takeaway:*** Rushing the design phase leads to costly changes later. Dedicate ample time here to ensure the plans perfectly reflect your vision.

## Phase 2: Permitting and Approvals (2–5 Months)

Once the design is complete, the plans must be submitted to the local jurisdiction (e.g., City of Bend, Deschutes County) and, often, the specific community HOA (e.g., Brasada Ranch, Tetherow). This is often the most variable component of the **custom home building timeline**.

Permit review times can fluctuate significantly based on the complexity of the design and the current workload of the planning department. For luxury, custom homes, the review is extensive. Rea Co Homes manages this entire process, leveraging our deep relationships with local authorities to streamline submissions and address queries quickly.

## Phase 3: Pre-Construction & Procurement (1–2 Months)

As permits are secured, the pre-construction phase focuses on logistical preparation and material procurement. This is vital for maintaining an efficient **construction schedule Oregon** wide, especially given current supply chain realities.

*   **Final Budgeting & Scheduling:** The final construction budget is locked in, and a detailed project schedule is created.
*   **Subcontractor Bidding:** Rea Co Homes secures the best local craftspeople and specialized trades.
*   **Long Lead-Time Item Orders:** Materials with extended delivery times (e.g., custom windows, specialized HVAC systems, high-end imported stone) are ordered immediately. This proactive step is crucial for answering the question, **how long to build luxury home** without unnecessary delays.

## Phase 4: Construction (10–18 Months)

This is the active building phase, where the structure takes shape. The duration depends heavily on the size, complexity, and finish level of the home. A typical luxury custom build in Central Oregon usually falls within this range.

### Key Construction Milestones:

1.  **Site Work & Foundation (1–2 Months):** Clearing the lot, grading, utility rough-ins, and pouring the foundation.
2.  **Framing & Sheathing (2–3 Months):** The skeleton of the home goes up. This is where you truly see the scale of the project.
3.  **Rough-Ins (2–3 Months):** Electrical, plumbing, HVAC, and low-voltage systems are installed within the walls and ceilings.
4.  **Exterior & Interior Finishes (4–8 Months):** Installation of roofing, siding, windows, insulation, drywall, painting, cabinetry, flooring, and final fixtures. This phase requires intense coordination and attention to detail, a hallmark of Rea Co Homes' award-winning work.

## Total Custom Home Building Timeline Summary

When calculating the full **custom home building timeline**, including all phases, a typical luxury custom home project with Rea Co Homes in Central Oregon generally takes **16 to 31 months** from the initial meeting to move-in. The wide range accounts for design complexity, permitting variability, and material selection.

| Permitting & Approvals | 2–5 Months |
