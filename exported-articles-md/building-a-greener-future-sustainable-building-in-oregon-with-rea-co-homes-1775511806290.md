## Key Pillars of Sustainable Building with Rea Co Homes

At Rea Co Homes, our commitment to **eco-friendly custom homes** is built upon several core principles:

### 1. Superior Energy Efficiency

Energy consumption is one of the largest environmental impacts of any home. Our **energy efficient homes Bend** are designed to significantly reduce heating and cooling demands. This is achieved through:

*   **Advanced Insulation:** Utilizing high-performance insulation in walls, roofs, and foundations to create a tight thermal envelope.
*   **High-Performance Windows:** Installing double or triple-pane windows with low-emissivity (Low-E) coatings to minimize heat transfer.
*   **Efficient HVAC Systems:** Incorporating high-efficiency heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems, often paired with smart thermostats for optimal control.
*   **Passive Solar Design:** Strategically orienting homes and placing windows to maximize natural light and warmth in winter, while minimizing heat gain in summer.
*   **Air Sealing:** Meticulous attention to sealing all gaps and cracks to prevent air leakage, a common source of energy waste.

These measures not only reduce your home's environmental footprint but also result in substantial savings on utility bills, making your investment in a sustainable home even more rewarding.

### 2. Responsible Material Sourcing

Choosing the right materials is crucial for **sustainable building in Oregon**. We prioritize materials that are:

*   **Locally Sourced:** Reducing transportation costs and emissions while supporting local economies.
*   **Recycled and Reclaimed:** Incorporating materials with recycled content or giving new life to reclaimed wood and other elements.
*   **Renewable and Sustainable:** Opting for rapidly renewable resources like bamboo or sustainably harvested timber.
*   **Low-VOC (Volatile Organic Compound):** Selecting paints, finishes, and adhesives that emit fewer harmful chemicals, contributing to better indoor air quality.
*   **Durable and Long-Lasting:** Investing in materials that stand the test of time, reducing the need for frequent replacements and minimizing waste.

### 3. Water Conservation

Water is a precious resource, especially in the high desert climate of Central Oregon. Our **eco-friendly custom homes** incorporate various water-saving strategies:

*   **Low-Flow Fixtures:** Installing toilets, showerheads, and faucets that reduce water consumption without sacrificing performance.
*   **Efficient Irrigation Systems:** Designing landscapes with native, drought-tolerant plants and smart irrigation systems that minimize water use.
*   **Rainwater Harvesting (Optional):** Implementing systems to collect and store rainwater for irrigation or other non-potable uses.

### 4. Healthy Indoor Environments

A sustainable home is also a healthy home. We focus on creating indoor environments that promote well-being by:

*   **Improving Air Quality:** Using low-VOC materials, advanced ventilation systems, and careful moisture management to prevent mold and mildew.
*   **Maximizing Natural Light:** Strategic window placement and open floor plans reduce the need for artificial lighting and connect occupants with the outdoors.
*   **Thermal Comfort:** Ensuring consistent temperatures throughout the home, eliminating drafts and hot spots.
