## The Path to a Solar Ready Custom Home

When planning a new build, future-proofing your investment is key. Rea Co Homes integrates features that make the transition to renewable energy seamless. We focus on designing a **solar ready custom home** from the foundation up.

This process involves more than just placing panels on the roof later. It requires careful planning during the design phase, including:

1.  **Optimal Orientation:** Positioning the home and rooflines to maximize solar gain potential.
2.  **Structural Integrity:** Ensuring the roof structure can handle the weight load of a full photovoltaic (PV) system.
3.  **Conduit and Wiring:** Installing necessary electrical conduits and dedicated circuits during framing, saving significant retrofit costs later.
4.  **Insulation and Air Sealing:** Maximizing the building envelope's performance so that any energy generated by solar panels is used as efficiently as possible.

Building a **solar ready custom home** with Rea Co Homes ensures your property is prepared for a sustainable future, whether you install the panels immediately or years down the line.

## Implementing Passive House Design Principles in Bend and Beyond

While achieving full Passive House certification can be complex, incorporating the core principles of **passive house design Bend** is a foundational element of Rea Co Homes' approach. Passive design focuses on using architectural elements and natural forces to maintain comfortable temperatures with minimal mechanical intervention.

Key elements of passive house design we integrate include:

*   **Super Insulation:** Utilizing advanced insulation materials (like continuous exterior insulation) and techniques to create a thermal blanket around the home.
*   **Airtight Construction:** Meticulous air sealing to prevent uncontrolled air leakage, which is a major source of energy loss.
*   **High-Performance Windows:** Installing triple-pane windows with low-emissivity coatings, specifically oriented to balance natural light and heat gain.
*   **Heat Recovery Ventilation (HRV) Systems:** Essential for maintaining excellent indoor air quality in airtight homes by exchanging stale air with fresh air while recovering heat energy.

Applying these principles, particularly crucial in areas like Pronghorn and Tumalo, results in a home that is incredibly quiet, comfortable, and requires up to 90% less energy for heating and cooling compared to conventional builds.

## Achieving Net Zero Home Central Oregon Goals

For clients committed to the highest level of sustainability, Rea Co Homes specializes in designing and constructing a **net zero home Central Oregon**. A net-zero home is one that produces as much energy as it consumes over the course of a year, typically achieved through a combination of extreme efficiency measures and on-site renewable energy generation (solar PV).

Achieving net zero requires absolute precision and commitment to detail. Kevin Rea's award-winning team manages the entire process, ensuring every component works in harmony:

1.  **Energy Modeling:** Detailed analysis during the design phase to predict energy usage accurately.
2.  **Advanced HVAC Systems:** Utilizing highly efficient systems like geothermal or high-efficiency heat pumps.
3.  **Water Conservation:** Integrating low-flow fixtures and efficient irrigation systems to reduce overall resource consumption.

Whether your project is a sprawling ranch in Sisters or a modern dwelling in the heart of Bend, we can guide you through the steps necessary to achieve a **net zero home Central Oregon**, ensuring your investment is both sustainable and luxurious.
