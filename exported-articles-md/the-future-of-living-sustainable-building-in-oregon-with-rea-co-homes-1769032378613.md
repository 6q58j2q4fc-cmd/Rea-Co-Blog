## Why Choose Energy Efficient Homes in Bend and Central Oregon?

The climate in Central Oregon presents specific challenges, from hot summers to cold, snowy winters. Building a standard home often results in high utility bills and inconsistent indoor comfort. This is why focusing on **energy efficient homes Bend** is crucial.

Rea Co Homes integrates advanced building science to maximize efficiency:

1.  **Superior Insulation and Air Sealing:** We utilize high R-value insulation and meticulous air sealing techniques to create a tight building envelope. This dramatically reduces energy loss, keeping the home cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter with minimal reliance on HVAC systems.
2.  **High-Efficiency HVAC Systems:** We often incorporate geothermal heating, high-efficiency heat pumps, and energy recovery ventilation (ERV) systems. These technologies ensure optimal air quality while using significantly less energy than traditional furnaces.
3.  **Passive Solar Design:** By carefully orienting the home and strategically placing windows, we maximize natural light and solar heat gain during winter months, reducing the need for artificial lighting and heating.
4.  **Water Conservation:** Given the high desert environment, water efficiency is key. We integrate low-flow fixtures, drought-tolerant landscaping (xeriscaping), and often incorporate rainwater harvesting systems.

## Defining Green Homes Central Oregon: Beyond Energy Efficiency

While energy efficiency is a core component, true sustainability encompasses much more. When we build **green homes Central Oregon**, we consider the entire lifecycle of the materials and the health of the occupants.

### Material Selection and Sourcing

We prioritize materials that are durable, locally sourced, and have low embodied energy. This includes:

*   **Reclaimed and Recycled Content:** Utilizing materials like reclaimed wood or recycled steel reduces the demand for virgin resources.
*   **Sustainable Wood Products:** Sourcing lumber from sustainably managed forests.
*   **Durability:** Choosing materials that resist the elements and require less maintenance over time, such as high-quality metal roofing and durable exterior finishes, is a cornerstone of **sustainable building Oregon**.

### Indoor Air Quality (IAQ)

An eco-friendly home is also a healthy home. We minimize the use of materials that off-gas harmful volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Low-VOC paints, finishes, and adhesives ensure that the air inside your custom home in Tetherow or Brasada Ranch is clean and safe for your family.
