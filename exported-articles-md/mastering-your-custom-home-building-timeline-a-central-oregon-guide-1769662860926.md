# Mastering Your Custom Home Building Timeline: A Central Oregon Guide

Building a custom luxury home in Central Oregon—whether nestled in the high desert of Bend, overlooking the Cascade Mountains in Tetherow, or enjoying the exclusive amenities of Brasada Ranch or Pronghorn—is an exciting journey. However, one of the most frequent questions we receive at Rea Co Homes is: "What is the **custom home building timeline**?" 

The answer is complex, dependent on design complexity, site conditions, and permitting processes unique to Deschutes County. Led by Kevin Rea, who brings over 45 years of award-winning experience to every project, Rea Co Homes specializes in demystifying this process. We believe clarity in the **construction schedule Oregon** is key to a successful outcome.

## Phase 1: Planning and Design (3–6 Months)

The initial phase is arguably the most critical and often takes longer than homeowners anticipate. This is where the vision comes to life on paper.

### Architectural Design and Engineering

We work closely with premier architects to translate your desires into detailed blueprints. For luxury properties, particularly those in exclusive communities like Tetherow or Brasada Ranch, architectural review board approvals are mandatory and can add time. Kevin Rea’s expertise ensures that the design process anticipates potential site challenges early on, saving time later.

### Budgeting and Financing

During this period, we finalize the comprehensive budget and specifications. Understanding the true cost upfront is essential. This phase concludes with securing financing and signing the construction contract.

## Phase 2: Permitting and Pre-Construction (2–4 Months)

Once the design is complete, the plans must be submitted for local approval. The timeline for permitting in Central Oregon varies significantly. Bend, Redmond, and Deschutes County have distinct processes. 

This phase is critical for determining **when to start building custom home**. While we wait for permits, Rea Co Homes focuses on detailed site preparation, including utility coordination, erosion control planning, and finalizing subcontractor schedules. Our proactive approach minimizes downtime once the permits are issued.

## Phase 3: Construction and Framing (4–6 Months)

With permits in hand, the physical work begins. This is the most visible and often fastest-moving part of the **custom home building timeline**.

### Site Work and Foundation

This involves excavation, laying the foundation, and installing underground utilities. Given the volcanic rock and high desert soil found throughout Central Oregon, proper site work requires specialized knowledge, which our team possesses.

### Framing and Rough-Ins

Once the structure is framed, the home truly takes shape. Following framing, the mechanical, electrical, and plumbing (MEP) systems are installed. This “rough-in” stage requires meticulous coordination to ensure all systems are perfectly aligned before walls are closed up. This phase is crucial in answering **how long to build luxury home**—complex designs with extensive smart home technology will naturally extend this period.

## Phase 4: Exterior and Interior Finishes (5–7 Months)

This phase focuses on weatherizing the structure and installing the high-end finishes that define a Rea Co Home.

### Exterior Finishes

Siding, roofing, windows, and exterior doors are installed, making the home weather-tight. For homes exposed to Central Oregon’s harsh winters and hot summers, material selection is paramount for durability and energy efficiency.

### Interior Details

This is where your selections shine: drywall, painting, cabinetry, flooring, tile work, and fixtures. For luxury builds, the installation of custom millwork, high-end appliances, and unique features like wine cellars or specialized home theaters requires precision and time. Our award-winning commitment to quality ensures every detail meets our rigorous standards.

## Phase 5: Final Inspections and Move-In (1 Month)

The final stretch involves detailed quality checks, final municipal inspections, and punch list completion. Rea Co Homes conducts multiple walkthroughs to ensure every element of the home meets the exact specifications and our high standard of craftsmanship. Once occupancy is granted, the home is professionally cleaned and ready for you.

## Total Custom Home Building Timeline Summary

For a typical high-end custom home in Central Oregon, the total duration from initial design meeting to move-in ranges from **14 to 24 months**. 

| Phase | Estimated Duration |
| :--- | :--- |
| Planning & Design | 3–6 Months |
| Permitting & Pre-Construction | 2–4 Months |
| Construction & Framing | 4–6 Months |
| Finishes & Detailing | 5–7 Months |
| **Total Estimated Time** | **14–24 Months** |
