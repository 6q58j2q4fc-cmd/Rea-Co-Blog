## Key Pillars of Energy Efficient Homes in Bend and Beyond

Creating **energy efficient homes Bend** and other Central Oregon communities relies on a multi-faceted approach. Rea Co Homes focuses on several critical areas to achieve superior energy performance:

### 1. Advanced Insulation and Air Sealing

The building envelope is paramount. We utilize high-performance insulation materials, including spray foam and rigid insulation, to create a thermal barrier that significantly reduces heat loss in winter and heat gain in summer. Meticulous air sealing techniques prevent drafts and uncontrolled air leakage, ensuring consistent indoor temperatures and reducing the workload on HVAC systems.

### 2. High-Performance Windows and Doors

Windows and doors are often weak points in a home's thermal performance. Rea Co Homes specifies high-quality, energy-efficient windows with features like low-emissivity (Low-E) coatings and argon gas fills. These components minimize heat transfer, protect interiors from UV radiation, and contribute significantly to overall energy savings.

### 3. Efficient HVAC Systems

Heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) are major energy consumers. We integrate high-efficiency furnaces, heat pumps, and energy recovery ventilators (ERVs) into our designs. These systems not only provide optimal indoor air quality and comfort but do so using a fraction of the energy of conventional units. Smart thermostats further optimize performance by learning homeowner preferences and adjusting settings accordingly.

### 4. Solar Energy Integration

For those looking to achieve near net-zero energy consumption, Rea Co Homes offers seamless integration of solar photovoltaic (PV) systems. Harnessing Central Oregon's abundant sunshine, solar panels can dramatically offset electricity usage, leading to substantial long-term savings and a reduced carbon footprint. This is a key aspect of truly **green homes Central Oregon** residents desire.

## Eco-Friendly Custom Homes: Materials and Practices

Beyond energy efficiency, our commitment to **eco-friendly custom homes** extends to the materials we select and the construction practices we employ:

### 1. Sustainable Sourcing

We prioritize materials that are locally sourced where possible, reducing transportation emissions. We also look for products made from recycled content, rapidly renewable resources (like bamboo), or those certified by organizations like the Forest Stewardship Council (FSC) for responsible forestry.

### 2. Waste Reduction and Recycling

Construction sites can generate significant waste. Rea Co Homes implements rigorous waste management plans, including comprehensive recycling programs for wood, metal, drywall, and other materials. Our goal is to divert as much waste as possible from landfills.

### 3. Healthy Indoor Air Quality

Sustainable homes aren't just good for the planet; they're good for their occupants. We select low-VOC (volatile organic compound) paints, finishes, and adhesives to minimize harmful off-gassing and ensure superior indoor air quality. Thoughtful ventilation strategies further enhance the health and comfort of your living environment.

### 4. Water Conservation

Water is a precious resource, especially in the high desert climate of Central Oregon. Our designs incorporate water-saving fixtures, such as low-flow toilets and showerheads, and can include options for drought-tolerant landscaping and rainwater harvesting systems, contributing to overall resource conservation.
