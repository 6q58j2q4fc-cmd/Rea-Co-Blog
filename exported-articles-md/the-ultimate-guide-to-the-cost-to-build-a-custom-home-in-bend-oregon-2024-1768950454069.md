## Key Factors Driving Luxury Home Building Costs

Several major components dictate the final investment required for your custom home:

### 1. Location and Site Preparation

The location significantly impacts the initial investment. Building in established, exclusive communities like Tetherow, Pronghorn, or Brasada Ranch often involves specific architectural review board requirements (ARBs) and higher impact fees. Furthermore, challenging terrain, the need for extensive rock removal (common in Central Oregon), or complex utility extensions can dramatically increase site development costs.

### 2. Architectural Complexity and Size

A simple, rectangular ranch-style home is inherently less expensive per square foot than a multi-story home with complex rooflines, numerous bump-outs, expansive glass walls, and unique structural requirements. The more complex the design, the higher the labor and engineering costs.

### 3. Materials and Finishes

This is where the distinction between a standard home and a luxury custom home is most apparent. Rea Co Homes is known for award-winning quality, which means utilizing premium materials:

*   **Exterior:** Natural stone veneers, imported siding, high-efficiency window packages (e.g., European tilt-and-turn).
*   **Interior:** Custom cabinetry, high-end appliance packages (Sub-Zero, Wolf), advanced smart home technology, radiant floor heating, and exotic hardwood flooring.

Choosing these high-specification items directly impacts the final **custom home price per square foot**.
