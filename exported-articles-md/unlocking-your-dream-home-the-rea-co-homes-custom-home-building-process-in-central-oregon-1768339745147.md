# Unlocking Your Dream Home: The Rea Co Homes Custom Home Building Process in Central Oregon

Building a custom home is one of life's most exciting and significant endeavors. It’s an opportunity to create a space that perfectly reflects your lifestyle, tastes, and aspirations. In the breathtaking landscapes of Central Oregon, from the vibrant city of Bend to the exclusive communities of Brasada Ranch, Tetherow, and Pronghorn, Rea Co Homes stands as the premier custom home builder dedicated to turning these dreams into reality. Under the seasoned leadership of Kevin Rea, with over 45 years of experience and a legacy of award-winning work, our **custom home building process** is designed for clarity, collaboration, and unparalleled quality.

Understanding the **construction process** can seem daunting, but with Rea Co Homes, it’s a transparent and enjoyable journey. We believe in open communication and meticulous planning, ensuring that every phase, from the initial sketch to the final polish, is executed with precision and care. Let’s explore the comprehensive steps involved in crafting your bespoke Central Oregon residence.

## Phase 1: Vision & Design – Laying the Foundation of Your Dream

The journey begins with your vision. This initial phase is crucial for understanding your desires, needs, and the unique characteristics of your chosen lot in Central Oregon. Whether you're envisioning a modern retreat in Bend, a rustic luxury home in Brasada Ranch, or a golf-course view residence in Tetherow, our team works closely with you to translate your ideas into a tangible design.

*   **Initial Consultation & Site Analysis:** We start by discussing your lifestyle, budget, and architectural preferences. Kevin Rea himself often participates in these early discussions, bringing his vast experience to bear. We'll also assess your chosen building site, considering factors like views, sun exposure, topography, and local zoning regulations unique to Central Oregon communities.
*   **Architectural Design & Floor Plans:** Collaborating with talented architects, we develop preliminary floor plans and elevations. This iterative process allows for adjustments and refinements until the design perfectly aligns with your vision. Our goal is to create a home that is not only beautiful but also highly functional and energy-efficient, leveraging the natural beauty of areas like Pronghorn.
*   **Material Selection & Specifications:** This is where your home truly begins to take shape. We guide you through selecting finishes, fixtures, and materials, from roofing and siding to cabinetry and flooring. Our expertise ensures that every choice contributes to the overall aesthetic and durability, while adhering to your budget and the high standards expected of a custom home builder.

## Phase 2: Pre-Construction & Permitting – Preparing for Takeoff

Once the design is finalized, the pre-construction phase focuses on all the necessary preparations before breaking ground. This is a critical step in ensuring a smooth **home building timeline**.

*   **Detailed Cost Estimation & Contract:** Based on the approved designs and material selections, we provide a comprehensive and transparent cost estimate. Once approved, a detailed contract outlining all aspects of the project, including scope, timeline, and payment schedules, is established.
*   **Permitting & Approvals:** Navigating the complex world of permits and local regulations can be challenging. Rea Co Homes handles all necessary applications, ensuring compliance with Central Oregon's specific building codes and environmental requirements. This includes everything from county permits to HOA approvals in communities like Tetherow or Brasada Ranch.
*   **Scheduling & Logistics:** With permits in hand, we develop a detailed project schedule, coordinating all trades, material deliveries, and inspections. Our meticulous planning minimizes delays and keeps the project on track.

## Phase 3: Construction – Bringing Your Vision to Life

This is the most visible and exciting phase, where your custom home begins to rise from the ground up. Our skilled craftsmen and trusted subcontractors, many of whom Kevin Rea has worked with for decades, are committed to excellence.

*   **Site Preparation & Foundation:** The site is prepared, and the foundation is laid with precision, forming the bedrock of your home's structural integrity.
*   **Framing & Rough-Ins:** The skeleton of your home takes shape with framing, followed by the installation of plumbing, electrical, and HVAC systems. Regular inspections ensure everything meets stringent quality and safety standards.
*   **Exterior & Interior Finishes:** Once the home is weather-tight, the focus shifts to the exterior (roofing, siding, windows) and interior finishes (insulation, drywall, cabinetry, flooring, paint). This is where the aesthetic details come alive, reflecting your personal style.
*   **Quality Control & Inspections:** Throughout the entire construction process, Kevin Rea and his project managers maintain rigorous quality control. Regular site visits and inspections by our team, alongside third-party inspectors, guarantee that every element meets our exceptionally high standards.

## Phase 4: Completion & Handover – Welcome Home!

The final phase culminates in the realization of your dream home, ready for you to move in and enjoy.
